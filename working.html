Design

Design for me means satisfying that appreciation one feels when everything just works. It means knowing the user and the back-end process so they work in tandem. A task can be complex but if each step is intuitive and inspiring the process will get done with ease. And when functionality combines with the aesthetic one can create a truly pleasing experience.

Technology

Change is the only constant, you have got to keep up or at least try. I am still amazed at the speed of progress in the world of web development. Keeping up with technological progress is a never ending task that must be done. To be a lifelong learner is now just a requirement of being a professional.

Smart Governance

Government is a reflection of a society and just as a mirror can show you a bad hair day ,government can display where a society needs to focus its efforts. By adhering to smart governance principles municipalities and larger institutions alike can help their populations to see clearly what needs attention. I strive to make government more efficient by improving the responsiveness to the governed.
